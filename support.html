Asteroid Support

Welcome to the Asteroid Support Center! We’re here to help you enjoy music without interruptions. If you have any questions, issues, or feedback, please reach out—we love hearing from our users. How to Get Help Technical Issues: If you experience bugs, crashes, or playback problems, please describe the issue in detail and include your device model and iOS version. Account Help: For login problems or account-related questions, provide your registered email. Feature Requests: Have ideas to make Asteroid even better? Let us know! Contact Us You can reach our support team via email: asteroidcontactbussiness@gmail.com We aim to respond within 24–48 hours. Thank you for using Asteroid and helping us support small artists and great music!
