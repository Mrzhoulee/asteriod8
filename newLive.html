🎤 How to go live (using YouTube Live)

1️⃣ Create a YouTube Live stream

  1. Go to YouTube Studio.
  2. Top right: Create → Go live, then choose Stream.
  3. Set your title, description and privacy (Public or Unlisted recommended).
  4. Click Create stream.
⚠️ Your channel must be verified and have live streaming enabled.

2️⃣ Get the embed code

  1. In YouTube Studio, edit your live stream from the Content tab.
  2. Scroll down and tick "Allow Embedding".
  3. Click Share → Embed and copy the code (it starts with <iframe …>).

3️⃣ Paste it on Asteroid

  1. Come back here and paste the full embed code into the box above.
  2. Double-check the whole code is pasted — don't edit it.

4️⃣ Click "Go Live"

  1. Pick a room and tap Go Live.
  2. Your YouTube stream appears automatically in your Asteroid concert room. 🎉

5️⃣ Share your room

Copy your concert room link and share it on Instagram, TikTok, Discord, or your YouTube description. Fans join → watch live → chat together.

✅ Tips

  1. Start the YouTube stream before clicking Go Live on Asteroid.
  2. Use Unlisted if you only want viewers from Asteroid.
  3. Make sure audio is working in YouTube Studio first.
